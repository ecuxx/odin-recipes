Lentil Soup

This is one of the most famous and traditional soups in Turkish cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups red lentil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Half a tablespoon of pepper or tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Half a tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 liters hot water
  • 5 tablespoons oil

    • For the sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper powder

Steps:

  1. Fry chopped onion in oil until it turns slightly pink.
  2. Add the flour and continue roasting on low heat.
  3. After roasting tomato paste, add washed and drained lentils.
  4. Add water, stir and close the pot lid. Cook on medium heat until the soup boils, then turn the heat to low and continue cooking until the lentils soften.
  5. Blend the sopu well with a blender.
  6. Add black pepper, and salt and mix. Cook for another 5 minutes and then remove from heat.
  7. Meanwhile, heat the butter in a small pan, add red pepper powder.
  8. After placing lentil soup in a serving bowl, pour the red pepper sauce over it and serve with a slice of lemon.
