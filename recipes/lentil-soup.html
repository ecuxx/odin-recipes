Lentil Soup

This is one of the most famous and traditional soups in Turkish cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups red lentil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • Half tablespoon of pepper or tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Half tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 liters hot water
  • 5 tablespoons oil

    • For the sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoons red pepper powder

Steps:

  1. Fry choppped onions with oil until they turn slightly pink.
  2. Add the flour and cotinue roasting on low heat.
  3. After roasting tomato paste well washed and drained lentils are added.
  4. Add water, stir and close teh pot lid. Cook on medium heat until the soup boils, then turn the low heat and continue cooking until the lentils are soften.
  5. Mash it well with a blender.
  6. Add black pepper, salt and mix. Cook for another 5 minutes and remove from heat.
  7. Meanwhile, heat the butter in a small pan, add red pepper powder.
  8. After putting lentil soup into a serving bowl, pour the red pepper sauce over it and serve with a slice of lemon.
