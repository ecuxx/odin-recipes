Flour Halva

This dessert is traditionally made after someone dies, and the final product indicating whether the person was good or bad, based on its density.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 125 grams butter
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1.5 cups water

Steps:

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the sugar and water. Stir until the sugar dissolves,then set aside for later use.
  2. Melt the butter in a separate pan and add the flour.
  3. Roast the flour for 10-15 minutes over medium heat, stirring costantly.
  4. When the flour changes the color, lower the heat and pour the cold syrup, stirring continuously.
  5. It will be very soft at first, but as you stir, the flour will absorb the syrup.
  6. Onces it reaches the right consistency, turn off the heat.
  7. Give a shape with 2 tablespoon.
