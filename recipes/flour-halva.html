Flour Halva

Usually cook after someone die and made decision if person was a good or bad depending on density of dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 125 grams butter
  • 1.5 cups of sugar
  • 1.5 cups of water

Steps:

  1. Put sugar and water in a medium heat and stir until the sugar dissolves. Keep aside to be used later.
  2. Melt butter and add flour.
  3. Roast it for 10-15 minutes in medium heat, while stirring costantly.
  4. When it change the color lower the heat and pour the cold syrup and stir continuously.
  5. It will be very soft at first but as you stir the flour will absorb the syrup.
  6. When it has reached the right consistency, turn off the heat.
  7. Give a shape with 2 tablespoon.
